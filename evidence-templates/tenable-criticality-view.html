How to Present This Context

This artifact exists to make triage prioritization operational instead of cosmetic.

  • Overview page anchor Use the criticality context to explain why the queue is ordered around business exposure, not just alert count.
  • Telemetry coverage credibility Show that the environment is not just collecting signals; it knows which assets are worth defending first.
  • Endpoint to MongoDB scenario support Pair the workstation and database rows to justify why the combined incident becomes materially more urgent.
Presenter cue: this is the easiest moment to explain why a high-confidence alert on a low-value asset may still rank below a medium-confidence event touching a critical business path.
